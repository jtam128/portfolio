About Me

My background in Software Development is not conventional. Before becoming a Software Developer, I worked as an Accountant. While working as an Accountant, I realized the important role that software played in our everyday life. It was from that point that I realized my interest in Software Development outgrew my interest in Accounting.

In order to pursue my interest in Software Development, I attended Thinkful's Software Engineering program where I learned full-stack development. This program was the beginning of my Software Engineering journey and I am looking forward to continuiously learn as well as applying the knowledge I gained into building lasting and impactful applications.
